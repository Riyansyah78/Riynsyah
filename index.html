Serve Tutorial

The Serve in Volleyball is the act the puts the ball into play, that is, which starts the “Rally” in Volleyball. The Serve is also called Service.

Passing Tutorial

Passing or baiting in volleyball is a technique of passing the ball to a teammate. Passing can be used as a pattern of defense and attack. opponent. The passing technique consists of two kinds, namely passing below and passing above.

Smash tutorial

Smash is a technique of attacking or hitting the ball in the air while jumping. The punches are carried out hard and dive which are directed to the opponent's area. The ball moves over the net and makes it difficult for the opponent to return it.
